Step1:

IDENTIFY A CAPITAL NEED

If you have questions concerning the qualifications of your grant request, please contact the foundation at info@hoblitzelle.org or by calling (214) 373-0462. If this is your first submission with the Foundation, we encourage you to discuss the potential grant opportunity prior to submitting an application. Please review the “What We Fund” section for current guidelines and funding interests, and note that grants are typically not made to an organization more than once every three to four years.

Step2:

APPLY FOR A GRANT

Hoblitzelle Foundation utilizes an online application process. In addition to general information about the organization, the specific capital request, and the budget, the following information is required to be submitted with your grant application:


  1. IRS Exemption Lette
  2. Organization’s most recent Form 990
  3. Organization’s most recent audited financial statements
  4. 4. List of Board of Directors
  5. Review of other financial support the Organizations has received over the past three years from other foundations and major donors.
